F–CUT: DESIGN YOUR VERY OWN FREITAG BAG

In the F–Cut, you can create your own F712 DRAGNET, almost like a bag designer at the FREITAG factory.

 

Starting now, you can fulfill your wildest bag dreams yourself, right here at the virtual cutting table, and have your very own super–unique model cut from the used truck tarps of your choice.

With four virtual templates and a choice of 20 to 50 different digitized truck tarps, you can start designing your own F712 DRAGNET messenger bag right now. And you can do so to suit your own taste, no matter how good, and ignoring all design guidelines.

OUR SWEAT–YOURSELF SHOPS

So far, with FREITAG, you’ve been able to simply pick out your ready–made one–off. But now, at our Sweat–Yourself Shops, you’re in charge of the final production stages as well as the entire design process.

 

Under the friendly but watchful eyes of the Shop crew, you can choose the various parts of the bag to suit your own taste, good or otherwise. For some models you might need a punching machine. Or you can use a cutter and perspex template to cut and design a small part of the bag using a tarp remnant from FREITAG production in Oerlikon.

 

Finally, you hand over your work to the practiced shop crew, who will sew or rivet it for you immediately or at some time later.

 

Some of our Sweat–Yourself offers are even available per video call.
